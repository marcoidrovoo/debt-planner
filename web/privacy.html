Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 1, 2026

Paycheck Plan is a simple budgeting tool. We do not require you to create an account to use the app.

Information We Collect

Local data only: The income, bills, and debts you enter are stored locally in your browser (localStorage or sessionStorage). This data does not leave your device.

Analytics

We currently do not collect analytics or tracking data. If this changes, we will update this policy.

Cookies

We do not use cookies for tracking.

Data Deletion

You can clear your data anytime using the “Clear Saved Data” button in the app or by clearing your browser storage.

Contact
